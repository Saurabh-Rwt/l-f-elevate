About #lyxel&flamingo

#lyxel&flamingo Elevate Summit is an invite-only conference for entrepreneurs, growth marketers, and product owners from consumer brands.It aims at helping you network with and learn from experts and leaders engaging customers and driving growth in a digital-first world.Building long-lasting customer relationships is the biggest growth lever. We invite leaders who share stories, discuss strategies, and teach frameworks on customer engagement and growth.

For more details, please reach out to register@lyxel&flamingo.com
